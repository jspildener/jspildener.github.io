About Me

Jessi has her masters degree in Pharmacology and Toxicology from the University of Toledo. She currently works in a molecular diagnostics labratory at the Cleveland Clinic as a technologist.

Some of Jessi's interests include painting, crocheting, cooking, and walking her three dogs. Jessi and her husband love exploring Cleveland, especially trying out all of the craft breweries in the area!
